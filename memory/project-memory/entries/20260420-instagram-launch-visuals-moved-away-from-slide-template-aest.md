## Details

Used Canva generation as a diagnostic step, but kept final production in the local generator because Canva without uploaded brand assets kept inventing the R mark. Updated scripts/generate_instagram_launch_assets.py to redesign post 1 as a stronger brand poster, post 2 as a photo-led feed creative without a button-like pill, carousel slide 1 to use the desk negative space for the 'Antes' message, slide 2 to separate it visually from post 2, and slide 3 to work as a cleaner editorial end card. Visual validation was done after each round on the generated PNGs.
