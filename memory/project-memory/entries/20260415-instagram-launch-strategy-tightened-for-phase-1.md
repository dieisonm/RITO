## Details

The growth plan for the RITO Instagram was tightened to fit the initial company stage and prepare for future paid media. The recommended launch sequence now emphasizes: 2 static posts, 1 short carousel, 2-3 support stories per publication, a single WhatsApp CTA, and a small hashtag set tailored to brand, niche, pain, and conversion intent. The documentation was updated in docs/company/presence/instagram-launch-strategy.md, operations/instagram/organic-growth-starter.md, operations/instagram/hashtag-bank.md, and operations/instagram/README.md.
